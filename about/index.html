About

Hello there I’m Claudio, a product designer freelancing from Monterrey, Mexico. I'm currently looking for product design opportunities focusing on design systems or internal business tools. View résumé.

I graduated with a BBA in Entrepreneurship from Baylor University and worked on my first design project as part of the Baylor Accelerated Ventures Program. Over the past couple of years I’ve freelanced as an interaction designer for Brushfire Interactive, worked as a web designer for Parámetro Studio, and helped businesses develop custom online experiences as a freelance web designer. My business studies and my design and development experiences have helped me become aware of the importance of automating and defining design systems to facilitate solving business and user needs.

Most recently I participated in Dann Petty's Epicurrence #5 in Jackson Hole, Wyoming where I had an opportunity to spend a week with some of the most talented designers and creatives. Over the holidays I participated in Holiday Office Hours, a mentorship program initiated by Dustin Senos that connects young designers with design and development leaders.

My goals for 2017 are to become a better writer, find an opportunity to join a design team, and dive into JavaScript web development.

Mind.log()

Exploring a (futuristic) idea on how to improve self awareness with biological equivalent of JavaScript's `console.log()`.

I struggle with overthinking. I'm always thinking and it's not usually about positive things. My mind tends to gravitate towards mistakes I've done, or events that are about to happen, or things I could have done. The mental noise my mind creates is never about the now and always about the past or the future. I've been journaling for many years now and it has definitely helped me realize how loud and persistent this mental noise is. Only by analyzing and reading through my journal entries have I become aware of this mental noise. I'd love to create something that logs all of my thoughts, both conscious and subconscious thoughts. That would be extremely enlightening. I would also like to create a program that analyzes those thoughts to track thinking patterns and how I tend to think about certain things either by time of day, by physical activity, by the people I'm surrounded by at the moment... mapping my thoughts to the external events that are influencing my thoughts. That would be a fun project to work on. It would be like JavaScript's `console.log()` but for the mind. It will silently run in the background as we go about our day.
